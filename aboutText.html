About the Game

This game is inspired by the popular online drawing and guessing game, Skribbl.io.

How to Play

  1. Two players take turns drawing and guessing words.
  2. The drawing player selects a word from the combo box and starts drawing it on the canvas.
  3. The guessing player has 5 attempts to guess the word by typing in the input box and submitting their guess.
  4. If the guess is correct, the guesser scores a point and the roles switch.
  5. After both players have drawn, the round ends, and a new round begins.

Enjoy the game and have fun!
